About Me

Hello! I'm Joã Swart, 21 years old and passionate about software development. I'm currently studying at MBO Rijnland in Gouda, where I'm actively developing my skills through hands-on projects, coursework, and self-learning. I enjoy experimenting with code, exploring different technologies, and finding creative solutions to real-world problems. Each project I work on is a new opportunity to grow and push my boundaries.

My goal is to combine technical skills with creativity to build innovative, user-friendly software. I'm particularly drawn to full-stack development and UI/UX design, where I can focus on both functionality and visual experience. I love creating interfaces that are not only clean and aesthetic but also intuitive and meaningful for users.

Outside of coding, I'm a big fan of gaming whether it's on my PC, in VR, or on mobile. I also enjoy playing badminton, watching movies, photography and traveling to experience new places and cultures.
